DIY Galaxy in a Jar

Materials Needed:

  • A clean glass jar (with a lid)
  • Cotton balls
  • Water
  • Blue and purple food coloring
  • Glitter (optional)
  • Glow-in-the-dark stars or stickers (optional)
  • A wooden stick or straw for mixing

Step-by-Step Instructions:

Step 1: Prepare the Jar

Take a clean glass jar and make sure the lid fits securely. Add a few cotton balls to the bottom of the jar. Make sure they are loosely placed, not too compact.

Step 2: Create the Galaxy Colors

In a separate cup, mix water with a few drops of blue food coloring. Pour some of the blue-colored water into the jar, letting it soak into the cotton balls.

Step 3: Add More Layers

Place another layer of cotton balls on top of the soaked ones. In a different cup, mix water with purple food coloring and pour this over the second layer of cotton.

Step 4: Add the Stars

Sprinkle some glitter on top of each layer to mimic stars and stardust. You can add glow-in-the-dark stars or stickers on the outside of the jar for extra space vibes.

Step 5: Seal the Galaxy

Once you've added enough layers of color and cotton, seal the jar tightly with the lid. Give the jar a gentle shake to distribute the colors and glitter evenly.

Step 6: Enjoy Your Mini Galaxy

Place the jar on a shelf or use it as a nightlight by placing it near a soft light source.
