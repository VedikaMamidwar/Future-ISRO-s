What is a Galaxy?

A galaxy is a massive system of stars, stellar remnants, interstellar gas, dust, and dark matter bound together by gravity.

Types of Galaxies

Galaxies can be classified into three main types: spiral galaxies, elliptical galaxies, and irregular galaxies.

Our Galaxy: The Milky Way

The Milky Way is a spiral galaxy that contains billions of stars, including our sun, and is part of the Local Group of galaxies.
