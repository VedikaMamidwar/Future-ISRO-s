What is a Satellite?

Satellites are objects placed into orbit around Earth or other celestial bodies for purposes such as communication, weather monitoring, and scientific research.

Types of Satellites

There are various types of satellites including communication satellites, weather satellites, and reconnaissance satellites.

ISRO's Satellites

ISRO has launched numerous satellites for various purposes, including the GSAT series for communication and the INSAT series for meteorology.
